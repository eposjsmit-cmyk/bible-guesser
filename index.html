Bible Guesser

Like GeoGuessr — but for the Bible.

  2. Click the map where you think it happened.
  3. Press Confirm before the timer runs out.
  4. The closer your guess, the more points — up to 5000.
  5. 5 rounds. Beat your best total!

A beep warns you in the last 5 seconds. Run out of time with no guess and it's 0 points!
